The barangay of Bulacan + San Juan is home to alot of unknown tourist spots all around it's borders, These are one of the popular tourist site's that are popular in the region but are unknown outside of it and beyond the world, Visit these spots to increase you're opinion on the nature of The Philippines and Realize how beautiful the country is.

Aloha Falls

Nueva Ecija

Aloha Falls is located in barangay Puncan in Carranglan, Nueva Ecija. Surrounded by the green foliage and about 15 meters to 20 meters in height. Aside from being picnic grove, the site is also perfect for trekking and mountain climbing.

Verdivia Falls

Bulacan

Rapid fall of water and birds chirping are the sounds you can hear at Verdivia Falls. The place is so peaceful. There are only few visitors and there are no wastes. The water is so clean and refreshing. You can climb to the top and jump on the cliff and jump and dip in the cold water of Verdivia Falls. The way to get there is a river where the waters come from the falls. On top of the falls, there is also a river where the water comes from. There are no tourist guide and you have to explore it all by yourself. Huge rocks are formed and you can climb on it. The trees are preserved and safe from logging. Verdivia Falls is a peaceful and amazing place.
